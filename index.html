Train Kid

Stop being late on the T. Get around faster, and know if you might be late ahead of time.

Using Train Kid

Train Kid is built as a mobile web app. Add it to your home screen - this makes it behave like a native app. Look for the "Add to Home Screen" option in your browsers menu (usually the three dots).
